Parking App Manual

This app is created to help users with their parking needs.

Sign In

This screen enable the users to sign In with their credentials if they've used the app before. Simply enter your login and password to access the app

Sign Up

First time users can create their account by signing up. You'll need to enter the following information in order to sign up successfully.

  • Name
  • Email
  • Password
  • Contact Information
  • Car Plate Number
  • Payment Information

Home Screen

This is where user is presented with all the necessary information to to park the vehicle

  1. Adding a Parking
    • Add the following Information:
    • Building code (each building will have a unique numerical code)
    • No. of hours intended to park (1-hour or less, 3-hour, 10-hour, 24-hour)
    • Car Plate Number
    • Suit no. of host
  2. First 3 parking in every month are free of cost.
  3. Other than that, charge is
    • $4 for 1-hour or less time
    • $8 for up to 3-hour time
    • $12 for up to 10-hour time
    • $20 daily max (24-hours) time
  4. Your receipt shows you all the details and charges for your parking.

Consumer Support

We're always more than happy to help you. For any feedback or inquiry user can go to the consumer support page and can contact us anytime. Our lines are open 24/7
